Medicine Overdose Prediction using Machine learning




About



Leveraging advanced data analytics and machine learning to predict and prevent medicine overdoses for improved patient safety.

To develop a predictive system that identifies patients at risk of medication overdose based on their medical history, prescription details, and other relevant factors. The system aims to assist healthcare providers in preventing overdoses by providing actionable insights and alerts during prescription and medication dispensation.
